Privacy Policy

Simple, Clear, and Transparent

We Do NOT Collect Your Data

This application (Flight Display) respects your privacy completely. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

What This Means

No Personal Information

We do not ask for or collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personal details.

No Usage Tracking

We do not track how you use Flight Display or monitor your activities.

No Cookies or Analytics

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, or any tracking technologies.

No Data Storage

We do not store any information about you on our servers or databases.

How Flight Display Works

Flight Display runs entirely on your device. Any data or settings you create stay on your device only. When you close the application, nothing is sent to us or stored anywhere else.

Think of it like using a calculator - we provide the tool, but we never see or keep track of what calculations you perform.

Your Data Stays Private

  • Local Storage Only: Any information stays on your device
  • No External Servers: We don't send your data anywhere
  • No Third Parties: We don't share with other companies
  • No Accounts Required: You don't need to sign up or log in

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will clearly announce it on this page. However, our commitment to not collecting your data will remain the same.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Flight Display, please feel free to contact us. We believe in being completely transparent about how we handle (or in this case, don't handle) your information.
